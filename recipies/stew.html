Beef Stew

Description

Beef Stew is a hearty mixture of solid foods and liquids, usually a gravy base or soup. The beef is stewed with carrots, potato, and sometimes celery and/or onion.

Ingredients

  • Stewing Beef
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • POtatoes
  • Broth

Steps

  1. Brown meat in oil
  2. Remove meat and saute veg
  3. Return meat to the pot
  4. Add broth
  5. Allow to cook at a low temperature until ingredients are tender
