Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A simple and flavour packed pasta dish that's easy to prepare

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Can of chopped tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Basil and Oregano
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a pan
  2. Remove the beef and saute onions and garlic
  3. Retrun the meat to the pan
  4. Add the canned tomatoes and season with basil and oregano
  5. Cook the spagghetti per packet instructions
  6. Add the coooked spagghetti to the bolognese sauce
