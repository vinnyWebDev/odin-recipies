Baked Salmon

Description

Salmon is a flavorsome fish, ideal for baking and rich in protein

Ingredients

  • Salmon Darnes
  • OLive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Lemon

Steps

  1. Lightly drizzle the oil over the salmon
  2. Season with salt and pepper/li>
  3. Bake in a preheated overn at 180c for 15 minutes
  4. Drizzle lemon over the cooked salmon
